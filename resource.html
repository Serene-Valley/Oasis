What do we do at Oasis?

Oasis is an organization that offers resources for individuals who are homeless or at risk of becoming homeless, regardless of their age. The organization recognizes that homelessness can affect individuals of any age, and therefore provides support and resources for everyone in need.For example, Oasis may provide emergency shelter for families with children, or offer job training and employment assistance for young adults who are experiencing homelessness.In addition to these services, Oasis may offer educational resources and assistance with finding stable housing for individuals who are experiencing homelessness. By offering resources for individuals of any age, Oasis strives to help everyone in need to overcome homelessness and achieve stability and independence.

Adult Resources

Free Healthcare and Insurance

Unfortunately, members of our homeless population suffer from chronic health issues, but due to their circumstances most of them are left with no help. At Oasis, anyone in need of healthcare will have access to our 24/7 team of healthcare professionals. This service is not limited and individuals in need will have access to dental, vision, primary health services and more.

Free Shelter

There are far too many vacant home in the city for anyone to be cold at night. Oasis offers safe and free shelter along with meals and other necessities. No one should be denied a home and at Oasis we have more than enough shelter for the ones in need.

Job Placement Opportunities

The members of Oasis see the drive and talent in our homeless populations. Oftentimes a lot of these individuals have dreams that they can achieve with some support. We believe in you all. Here, we aim to do more than just give you a bed to sleep on, but rather give you the opportunity to own your own.

Education for All, Vocational Education

At Oasis any adult whom desires to either continue their education or take on a different trade will be offered an abundance of support and free classes. Continuing your education can help open you up to numerous opportunities to help you get back on your feet. Oasis can help you prepare for your next journey. We have eliminated the screening and financial barriers that would otherwise stop you from being able to come out of your situation.



Child Resources

Education for All

When a child has to worry about where their next meal is coming from, being in school can often be the last thing on their mind. At Oasis we want to help change their reality. Studies have found that between 63-90% of homeless youth wont finish high school, even though they were the age to do so. The schools at Oasis are aware of this problem and have additional targeted resources to help homeless children stay in school.

Free Healthcare!

Navigating the healthcare system without a guardian or parent present at times can be hard. Regardless of whether you are able to bring in a guardian or not, our team of healthcare professionals will do our best to ensure that you are healthy despite your challenges. Not only will our healthcare team do our best to make sure that you are healthy, we are open 24/7 and free for you to use.

Mental Healthcare

Being homeless not only has negative health impacts on our body, but our mind as well. Oasis has curated a team of dedicated mental heath professionals and advocates for children and teens to help you navigate these hard times. Our mental health services are free of cost to you. Children who experience homelessness have a significantly higher chance of developing long-term mental health issues, our team is here to help.

Housing, Safe Havens

The members at Oasis understand how dangerous and scary the world is. No child should have to worry about where they'll be sleeping every night, that why Oasis has a dedicated team will to care for and look after the children our society often neglects. Shelter at Oasis looks like proper meals, haircare, clothing, privacy, necessities, and most importantly caring (and more!).
